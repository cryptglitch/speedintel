How to Improve Your Speed
📍 Router PlacementCentral, elevated, away from walls & microwaves for best Wi-Fi coverage.
🔌 Use EthernetWired connections are faster and more stable than Wi-Fi for desktop use.
🔄 Restart RouterMonthly reboots clear cache and often improve speeds significantly.
📶 Change Wi-Fi Band5GHz is faster for close devices; 2.4GHz penetrates walls better.
🛡️ Check for InterferenceNeighbours' Wi-Fi, cordless phones, and baby monitors cause slowdowns.
🌐 Use DNS ServersCloudflare (1.1.1.1) or Google (8.8.8.8) can reduce ping noticeably.
