Optimisation Tips
📍 Router placementCentral, elevated, away from walls and microwaves.
🔌 Use ethernetWired connections are faster and more stable than Wi-Fi.
🔄 Restart routerMonthly reboots clear cache and often improve speeds.
📶 Switch Wi-Fi band5 GHz is faster nearby; 2.4 GHz penetrates walls better.
🛡 Reduce interferenceCordless phones and baby monitors slow Wi-Fi.
🌐 Use fast DNSCloudflare 1.1.1.1 or Google 8.8.8.8 can reduce ping.
